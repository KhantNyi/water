Quick Payout Estimator

Enter your type + weight. This is a demo UI (no backend yet).

Example rates (demo): Plastic ฿8/kg • Paper ฿3/kg • Aluminum ฿35/kg • Glass ฿1/kg

1) List your recyclables

Choose waste type, add estimated weight, and see a rough payout instantly.

2) Book a pickup

Select a date/time window. We assign a nearby driver/collector.

3) Verify & get paid

Collector weighs on arrival, confirms rate, and you get paid via cash/transfer.

Transparent pricing

Prices vary by market rate, cleanliness, and sorting quality. WasteBuy shows an estimate first, then confirms the final payout after weighing. For a student demo, you can keep rates fixed and show the “final weigh-in” step as a confirmation screen.
